Our Services

Comprehensive Construction Solutions

01

Complete Stupa Construction

We handle new Stupa projects from the ground up ("Peshawa" to "Koth Kerella"). Our approach ensures structural stability using high-grade reinforced concrete or traditional brickwork as requested.

02

Restoration & Waterproofing

Specialized repair of cracks in ancient Stupas. We use modern waterproofing chemicals combined with traditional lime plasters to ensure the Stupa remains white and protected for years.

03

Kotha & Chudamanikkaya Mounting

The pinnacle placement is the most sacred moment. We have specialized equipment and experienced teams to safely hoist and install the "Chudamanikkaya" (Crystal) and "Kotha".

04

Wahalkada & Architectural Details

Detailed construction of Wahalkada (gateways), Moonstones, and other ornamental features that enhance the beauty of the Stupa complex.
