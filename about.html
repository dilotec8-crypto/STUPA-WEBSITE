A Journey of Devotion

Founded over three decades ago, W.G. Siripala Gamage Stupa Construction Services began with a singular vision: to preserve the ancient art of Stupa construction in Sri Lanka. What started as a small team of dedicated craftsmen has grown into a renowned construction service trusted by temples island-wide.

Use of traditional "Hunu" based mixtures or modern concrete technology, we adapt to the request while maintaining the spiritual guidelines (Vasthu) that make these structures sacred.

"Building a Stupa is not just construction; it is an act of merit that echoes through eternity."

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and spiritually conscious construction partner for Buddhist heritage sites in Sri Lanka.

Our Mission

To combine ancient architectural wisdom with modern engineering excellence to build durable, majestic Stupas for future generations.
